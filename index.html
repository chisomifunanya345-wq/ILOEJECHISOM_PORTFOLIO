ProActive Prosthetics Clinic

ProActive Prosthetics Clinic will be buit as a modern, responsive web platform designed to provide accessible and patient-centered prosthetic and orthotic care services. The website will allow users to easily book appointmentswith certified prosthetists, access online consultations from the comfort of their homes, and order a range of orthotic devices such as braces, supports, rehabiltational aids

The platform will be built to simulate a real-world healthcare system that enhances mobility care delivery through digital solutions. It will be designed with a clean, user-friendly interface that ensures patients can navigate services effortlessly, request professional support, and receive timely assistance.

Clubfoot Care & Awareness Platform

Clubfoot Care & Awareness Platform will be a responsive healthcare website designed to educate and support individuals affected by clubfoot. The platform will provide comprehensive information on the condition, including causes, early diagnosis, and treatment options such as the Ponseti method

It also offers features that allow users to book consultations with specialists, access guidance for home care, and explore orthotic treatment options like corrective braces. The website will be built to simplify complex medical information into a clear and user-friendly experience, making it accessible to parents, caregivers, and patients. This project highlights the use of web design in healthcare education and patient support, focusing on accessibility, clarity, and real-world application.
